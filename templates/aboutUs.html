About Us


With Sunlight Tea, it’s all about intensifying your tea experience

5 years of history

Imagine living in a world where a better source of tea, with fresher products, and easier shopping was available. That’s what our aim is at sunlight tea. Our loose leaf teas comes straight from local farmers in a variety of blends and brew methods. By sourcing directly, we’re able to share these exquisite teas with minimal markup.Each cup contains the promise of a new journey, full of wonder and peace. So if you’re looking for higher-quality loose tea, Sunlight Tea is where you should start.
